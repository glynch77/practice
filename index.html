About Me

I’m a certified personal trainer with a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology and a strong passion for helping people move, feel, and live better. I was born and raised in Oklahoma, where I learned the value of hard work, consistency, and taking care of your body for the long run. My training approach is rooted in science, focusing on proper movement, strength, and injury prevention. I don’t believe in quick fixes or extreme programs — I believe in building habits that actually last.

Outside the gym, I’m a husband and a proud father of three, which keeps me grounded and motivated every day. Being a family man has taught me the importance of balance, time efficiency, and realistic fitness goals. I understand how busy life can get, and I design training programs that fit into real schedules. Whether your goal is to get stronger, move better, lose weight, or simply feel more confident, I meet you where you are. My mission is to help you build a healthier lifestyle that supports both your fitness goals and your everyday life.
